### May 1: Bike Ride Through The Teltowkanal
![Teltowkanal picture](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/d/df/Teltowkanal_beim_Wulfila_Ufer_%28Teltow_Canal_by_Wulfila_Bank%29_-_geo.hlipp.de_-_29734.jpg)

I went with my father. It was our first bike trip here in Teltow and it didn't let me down. Yes, it was city and there were a bunch of rules of the road I didn't road, but the cycle way that went along the river was refreshing and had beautiful sights. In total, I rode **11.6km** in a span of **1 hour**. I would *definetely* repeat this.
